# ◼ INTRO

This project is started with an idea...

- 'How to manage the test results of the highschool students uncomplicately?'
- 'What if there is a service to lead students and teachers are able to consult their future?'
We began to develop the idea

### ⇒  The service that managing their academic performance maximize!

</br>


## ◼ Targets

- **Highschool students** in Daegu, South Korea aiming to apply higher education institution 
- **Teachers** wishing to manage their student's academic result **efficiently and systematically**
- Highschool administartive staffs

# ◼ Key Functions

**1. Providing results way more easliy and intutitively!** <br>
    - Student : Highest and latest results of the main subjects<br>
    - Teacher : Average grade of the class (Mock/Aca)<br>
    - can check it out the transitions intutitively by the visual graph <br>
    
 **2. TimeTable**: Providing weekly timetable Using public Api provided by Ministry of Education in South Korea <br><br>
 **3. LunchTable**: Weekly&monthly lunchtable with public Api<br><br>
 **4. ScheduleTable**: Weekly schedule of the school for teachers<br><br>

 **5. Automatic Aca result calculating**<br>
    - Auto Calculating school ranking and grade<br>
    - Auto Calculating class ranking<br>
    ※followed the grading criteria of South Korea education ministry<br><br>
    
 **6. School records**<br>
    - providing attendence records by grade management page<br>
    - Career consulting records page <br><br>
 **7. Download result records by period**<br>
    - Excel form<br><br>
 **8. (admin) Announcement board & management of teacher accounts of the school**<br>
